WORKS ABOUT

Hello,

My name is Shuhan Yu, and I am a digital artist and painter studying at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts. My work examines the relationships between body, space, and environment across multimedia.

I have produced 2D & 3D animations, oil paintings, video installations, artist books, short films, and zines, utilizing a diverse array of industry-standard software including Autodesk Maya, Unreal Engine, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Storyboard Pro.

This portfolio presents selected works from my studies and projects. I am open to freelance / internship opportunities and collaborations in digital art, 2D & 3D animation, galleries and mixed media.
