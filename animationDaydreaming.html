Project Description

This project is inspired by a dream. Through fragments of memory, I attempt to decipher its language, tracing how dreams fold into recollection and how recollection, in turn, becomes a quiet cycle of life and return. It is a meditation on the fragile link between the small and the immense, the intimate and the infinite.

And in dreams, falling becomes the only way to tell what is real.

Year: 2025  |  Medium: 3D Animation (Unreal Engine, Autodesk Maya, Adobe Premier Pro)
